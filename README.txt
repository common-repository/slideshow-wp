== Installation ==

1. Upload `slideshow-wp.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `[sswp-slide sswpid=1]` in your posts or page OR
1. Place `<?php echo do_shortcode('[sswp-slide sswpid=1]');?>` in your templates
